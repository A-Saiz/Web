About me

Who I am

It has taken a long time to figure out who I am and what I want to do with my life. Salt Lake City, Utah in 1975 is where I started my strange and wonderful life.

Even as a child I knew I wanted my name to be known. With that thought in my head I tried my hand in many different things, but always went back to drawing. As I was growing up I learned about many different artists and how unique they are which made me realize that this is where I belong, creating beautiful things to make my mark in this world we live in.

My mission

Creating robust, reusable, and beautiful UI components and websites for ease of access that are visually appealing for all who visit.
